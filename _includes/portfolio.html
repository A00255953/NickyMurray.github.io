Escape 51 is an endless runner / non stop shooter for Android available in the play store. Portal Knight

Portal Knight is an endless platformer with a twist! The platforms are circular, and expand and rotate away from the centre points. Portal Knight was the inspiration for the Circle Renderer tool I created in unity. Check out portal knight here

Unity Tool
Circle Renderer

Circle Renderer is a Unity Editor extension that extends the line renderer component to draw circles and many other shapes. The tool also gives you the ability to add an Edge Collider to the generated shapes.
