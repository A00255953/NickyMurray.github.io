About

I am a mature student studying Software Design in Athlone Institute of Technology. Before deciding to get back into education to fulfill my lifegoal of becoming a software developer, I was in the British Army for 4 years in which I was trained as a Tank Driver and as part of that I undertook an apprenticeship in vehicle maintenance (aka a Mechanic). In my spare time I am either out exploring Ireland with my wife and daughter or in the gym. I also spend way too much time making apps and games for me and my family.

BudgApp

I created BudgApp for my wife to make doing the monthly finances quicker and painless. The original version was made using Unity because I knew how to use it well and decided I could throw a simple non-game related app together pretty quick. A couple months later I realised it was a terrible idea to use Unity between the non native UI components and the splash screen, so I decided to teach myself how to use Xamarin and its Mark-up language XAML. Check it out on GitHub here.
